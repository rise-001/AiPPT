---
title: "Manage Projects"
description: "View, rename, and delete past projects"
---

Click **History** in the top navigation bar to see all your presentations.

## Continue Editing a Project

Click any project card to jump back into that project and continue where you left off.

## Rename a Project

Hover over the project title — when the edit icon appears, click it, type the new name, then press **Ctrl+Enter** to save or **Esc** to cancel.

## Delete Projects

- **Delete one**: Click the delete icon on the card and confirm
- **Delete multiple**: Check several projects and click **Batch Delete**

<Warning>
  Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone — all pages, descriptions, and generated images are removed.
</Warning>
